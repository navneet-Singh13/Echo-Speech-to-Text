Introduction to Echo Speech-to-Text Tool for the Visually Impaired


This Speech-to-Text Tool is designed to empower blind and visually impaired individuals by converting spoken words into written text. Its features include:


  • Real-Time Voice Transcription: Instantly converts speech into text, ensuring a seamless experience for capturing ideas and conversations.
  • Multi-Language Support: Allows users to select their preferred language, catering to diverse linguistic needs.
  • Easy-to-Use Interface: Features a simple design with clear audio cues, minimizing visual complexity for enhanced accessibility.
  • Downloadable Text Files: Enables users to save transcriptions as .txt files for future reference or sharing.
  • Error Handling and Guidance: Provides clear audio alerts for issues such as:
    • Microphone problems.
    • No speech detection.
  • Enhanced Accessibility: Designed to boost independence and productivity for blind individuals, supporting tasks in personal, educational, and professional settings.


This tool represents a step toward inclusive technology, prioritizing accessibility and equality for all users.
